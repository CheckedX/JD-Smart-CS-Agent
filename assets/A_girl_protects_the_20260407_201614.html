A girl protects the forest animals

A story about Luna

It is a sunny weekend. Luna runs to the forest to play with her animal friends as usual, but she finds many big trees have been cut down. The little squirrel cries on the stump, and the rabbit and deer hide behind the small bushes in fear.

Kind Luna feels sad for the scared little animals. She carries them to her backyard, feeds them carrots and nuts, builds soft temporary nests for them, then tells her parents what happened, and they go to the community together to tell everyone about the animals' problem and ask for help.
